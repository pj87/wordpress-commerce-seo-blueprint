# SEO Affiliate Blog Starter

## Goal

Build a WordPress blog structured for search traffic, topic clusters, and monetization.

## Example

Best WordPress tools for small business websites.

## Pages

- Home
- Blog
- Category pages
- About
- Contact
- Affiliate disclosure
- Privacy policy

## Content Types

- How-to guides
- Best tools lists
- Comparison articles
- Checklists
- Tutorials
- FAQ articles

## Build Order

1. Choose niche and audience.
2. Define pillar topic.
3. Create 3 to 5 categories.
4. Plan 10 article titles.
5. Publish 3 complete articles.
6. Add affiliate disclosure.
7. Add internal links.
8. Add CTA blocks.
9. Configure SEO metadata and sitemap.
10. Review indexing and analytics.

## Done Criteria

- Blog has a clear niche.
- Categories support topic clusters.
- Articles match search intent.
- Affiliate links are disclosed.
- Internal links connect related articles.
