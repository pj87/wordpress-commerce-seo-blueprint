# Local Business Site Starter

## Goal

Build a WordPress website for a local business that needs credibility, local SEO, and lead generation.

## Example

Local plumber, dentist, restaurant, fitness trainer, consultant, or repair service.

## Pages

- Home
- Services
- Service detail pages
- About
- Reviews or testimonials
- Blog
- Contact
- Privacy policy

## Content Model

- Services
- Locations served
- Reviews
- FAQs
- Blog posts

## Recommended Plugins

- SEO plugin
- Contact form plugin
- Cache/performance plugin
- Security plugin
- Backup plugin
- Analytics integration

## Build Order

1. Discovery
2. Sitemap
3. Homepage
4. Services page
5. Contact page
6. Blog setup
7. Local SEO metadata
8. Forms and analytics
9. Launch checklist

## Done Criteria

- Visitor understands the offer within the first screen.
- Contact method is obvious.
- Service area is clear.
- Main pages have SEO titles and descriptions.
- Contact form works.
- Site is mobile-ready.
