# Sitemap And Content Workflow

## Goal

Turn discovery notes into a buildable sitemap and content plan.

## Inputs

- Discovery notes
- Required pages
- Service or product list
- SEO keyword ideas
- Existing content
- Brand assets

## Steps

1. Create the sitemap.
2. Assign one goal to each page.
3. Assign one primary keyword or search intent to each SEO page.
4. Define navigation and footer links.
5. Draft homepage sections.
6. Draft service, product, and category page outlines.
7. Plan blog categories and initial article titles.
8. Identify content missing from the client or project owner.

## Outputs

- Sitemap
- Page goals
- Navigation structure
- Footer structure
- Content inventory
- Initial SEO content plan

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every page has a purpose.
- Navigation supports the main conversion goal.
- SEO pages have clear intent.
- Missing content is documented.
