# Discovery Workflow

## Goal

Define the business, audience, offer, pages, features, and launch constraints before building.

## Inputs

- Business name
- Business type
- Primary offer
- Target audience
- Competitors
- Existing website if any
- Domain and hosting status
- Required integrations
- Launch deadline

## Steps

1. Identify the primary conversion goal.
2. Define the main audience and their buying/search intent.
3. List core services, products, or content categories.
4. Review 3 to 5 competitor websites.
5. Choose the project type: service site, SEO blog, WooCommerce store, or headless build.
6. Define required pages and features.
7. Confirm legal pages and compliance needs.
8. Confirm who owns content, images, domain, hosting, analytics, and Search Console.

## Outputs

- Project goal
- Target audience
- Required pages
- Required features
- Content owner list
- Technical constraints
- Launch checklist draft

## Acceptance Criteria

- The primary CTA is clear.
- Required pages are known.
- Content gaps are visible.
- Hosting and domain status are known.
- No major unknowns block the build.
