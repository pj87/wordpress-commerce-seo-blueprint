## Intro

WooCommerce is a strong fit for small stores when the product catalog, checkout, shipping, taxes, and policies are planned clearly.

Use this checklist before adding products.

## Store Setup

Configure:

- store address
- currency
- tax settings
- shipping zones
- payment methods
- customer accounts
- order emails

## Product Content

For each product:

- product name
- short description
- long description
- price
- SKU if needed
- category
- product images
- image alt text
- SEO title
- meta description

Internal link:

- Link product education articles back to `/services/` or `/contact/`.

## Checkout Testing

Before launch:

- add product to cart
- update quantity
- remove product
- complete test checkout
- confirm customer email
- confirm admin email
- check order status
- test refund workflow if available

## Store Policies

Add:

- terms and conditions
- privacy policy
- refund policy
- shipping policy
- contact information

## FAQ

### Should a small store customize checkout immediately?

Usually no. Start with standard WooCommerce checkout, test the purchase flow, then customize only if there is a clear reason.

### How many products are enough for a demo store?

Five simple products and two variable products are enough to test the core catalog and checkout flow.
