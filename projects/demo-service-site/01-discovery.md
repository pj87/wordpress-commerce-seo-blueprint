# Discovery

## Business

Name: WP Growth Studio

Business type: WordPress website service for local service businesses.

## Primary Goal

Generate consultation requests from business owners who need a faster, easier-to-edit WordPress site.

## Primary Audience

Small business owners, consultants, and local service providers who need:

- A better website
- More organic traffic
- Faster page loading
- A blog they can edit
- A basic shop or booking path later

## Primary Offer

Fixed-scope WordPress launch package:

- Conversion homepage
- Core business pages
- SEO blog setup
- Contact form
- Analytics and sitemap
- Performance and security basics

## Primary CTA

Book a consultation.

## Secondary CTA

View the launch checklist.

## Competitor Review Notes

Common competitor weaknesses:

- Generic design claims
- Unclear package scope
- Weak proof
- No visible process
- No SEO blog strategy
- No maintenance explanation

Positioning opportunity:

- Sell clear scope, fast launch, SEO structure, and maintenance readiness.
